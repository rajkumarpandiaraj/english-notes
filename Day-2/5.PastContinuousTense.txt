                                    5.Past Continuous Tense

He
She     was + verb + ing
It      was + not + verb + ing
I

We
You     were + verb + ing / were + not + verb + ing
They

EXAMPLE :
    she saw an accident when she was going to home yesterday.
    he was not sleeping.
    was he sleeping?
        use simple past for asking questions
            didn't he sleep?
