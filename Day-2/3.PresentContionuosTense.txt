                    3.Present continuous Tense[Used for currently doing things]

He
She  is + verb + ing/ is + not + verb + ing
It

EXAMPLE :
    He is writing a letter now[positive statement]
    He is not writing a letter now [negative statement].
    Is he writing a letter now? [positive question].
    Is she not writing a letter now? [negative question].

I am + verb + ing / am + not + verb + ing

EXAMPLE : 
    I am watching Tv now

We
You     are + verb + ing / are + not + verb + ing
They

EXAMPLE :
    They are playing now
    They are not playing now
    Are they playing now ?
    Are they not playing now ?
