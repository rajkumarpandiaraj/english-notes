                                 2.Simple Past tense

When to use Past tense :
    For All Past Actions :
        Positive Statement :
            He,she,It,I,we,You,They --> s + past form of Verb[v2].
        Example :
            I went to temple yesterday.
            She went to temple yesterday.
        Negative Statement :
            He,she,It,I,we,You,They --> s + didnot/didn't + Verb.
        Example :
            She didn't go to temple yesterday.
            They did not go to temple yesterday
        Questioning Statement 
            Positive :
                Did She go to temple yesterday?
                Did you go to school yesterday?
            Negative :
                Didn't she go to temple yesterday?
                Didn't you go to school yesterday?
