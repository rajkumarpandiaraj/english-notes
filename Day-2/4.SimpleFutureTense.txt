                            4.Simple Future Tense

He
She     is + going to + verb
It

I       am + going to + verb

We
You     are + going to + verb
They

He
She
It
I       will + verb
We
You
They

EXAMPLE :
    she is going to buy a car next week [+]
    she is not going to buy a car next week [-]
    is she going to buy a car?[?]

    I am going to sleep[+]
    I am not going to sleep[-]
    are you going to sleep?[?]

    I'll come tomorrow
    I'll not come tomorrow
    will you come tomorrow?

    I'm going to delhi by tomorrow [we can use present continuous tense in future tense also]
